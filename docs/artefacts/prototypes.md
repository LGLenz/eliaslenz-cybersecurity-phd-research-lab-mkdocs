## Policy-as-code guardrails

Planned and ongoing work includes:

- expressing selected governance requirements as PaC rules
- grouping and tagging guardrails for different audiences and contexts
- assessing LLM-assisted authoring and refactoring of guardrails
- exploring feedback channels (dashboards, reports, PR comments)

Where appropriate, generic or anonymised artefacts may be published either via
this lab site or attached to working papers.

## Tooling ecosystem

The broader tooling landscape may include:

- IaC scanning tools (e.g. for Terraform/Kubernetes)
- policy engines (e.g. OPA/Rego) and guardrail frameworks
- SBOM and dependency analysis tools
- experiment notebooks and simple web dashboards

The goal is not to build a monolithic tool, but to understand and document how
guardrails can be introduced in realistic environments.
